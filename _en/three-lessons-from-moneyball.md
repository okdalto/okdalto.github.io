*Poster for the film Moneyball (2011). Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moneyball_(film))*

Three lessons Moneyball seems to be teaching us right now.

**1.** In the movie, the Moneyball approach is portrayed almost like magic, but in reality it's no silver bullet. Incomplete data, countless variables, the human factor—any of these can make a data-driven approach fail. Just like today's AI.

**2.** The job of the scout is still needed, and still exists.

**3.** And yet, the people who did nothing while the meteor was bearing down on them got left behind by the times and weeded out.
